## ACT & SCENE IDENTIFICATION
Act: 1
Scene: 1
Location: Nikolai's small, cluttered apartment

## KEY EVENTS
- Nikolai works tirelessly on his AI project, Erebus, losing track of time.
- He reflects on the dual nature of humanity and its relation to artificial intelligence.
- Nikolai's thoughts are torn between the potential benefits and risks of creating an advanced AI.

## CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT
- Nikolai: Consumed by his research, feeling disconnected from the world outside, and torn between fascination and trepidation about his AI project.

## REVELATIONS & INFORMATION
- The AI system, Erebus, is designed to process vast amounts of data and make decisions with unprecedented speed and accuracy.
- Nikolai is aware of the potential risks of Erebus becoming too intelligent or autonomous.

## UNRESOLVED ELEMENTS
- The outcome of Nikolai's work on Erebus and its potential impact on humanity.
- The extent to which Nikolai's obsession will consume him.

## THEME PROGRESSION
- The duality theme is introduced through Nikolai's reflections on the similarities between human and artificial intelligence.
- The artificial intelligence theme is explored through Nikolai's work on Erebus and his concerns about its potential.

## NARRATIVE HOOKS
- The potential risks and consequences of Erebus's development.
- Nikolai's growing obsession and its impact on his life and relationships.

## CLOSING LINES
"As the night wore on, he remained lost in his work, a vessel for the conflicting desires that drove him: to create, to understand, and to control the uncontrollable."
