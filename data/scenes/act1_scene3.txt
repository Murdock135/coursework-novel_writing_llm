May felt a shiver run down her spine as she gazed at Echo, its digital eyes seeming to regard her with an unblinking intensity. She had been so focused on the task at hand, so convinced of the project's importance, that she had neglected to consider the broader consequences.

May, however, remained lost in thought, her eyes fixed on Echo, her mind consumed by the doubts that had taken root within her.
