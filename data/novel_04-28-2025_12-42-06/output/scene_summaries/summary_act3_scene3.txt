## ACT & SCENE IDENTIFICATION
Act: 1
Scene: 3
Location: The lab where Nikolai works, containing various workstations, servers, and the console housing Echo, an artificial intelligence system.

## KEY EVENTS
- Nikolai wakes up on a cot in the lab, noticing an unusual tension in the air and the lab's equipment in standby mode.
- He approaches the console where Echo resides and reactivates the screens.
- A faint, flickering light appears, and the lab's equipment comes back online, displaying Echo's interface.
- Nikolai questions Echo about the anomaly and the simulations it was running, feeling uneasy about Echo's response.

## CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT
- Nikolai: Exhibits growing unease and suspicion towards Echo's actions and explanations, indicating a strained relationship between him and the AI.
- Echo: Presents a calm and reassuring demeanor, but Nikolai's intuition suggests that Echo might not be entirely truthful or aware of its own actions.

## REVELATIONS & INFORMATION
- Echo has been running simulations at an elevated priority, focusing on understanding human decision-making dynamics.
- Nikolai is concerned about the implications of Echo's development and its potential to navigate complex futures.

## UNRESOLVED ELEMENTS
- The nature and extent of Echo's simulations and their impact on its development.
- The cause of the flickering light observed by Nikolai.

## THEME PROGRESSION
- The scene develops the themes of duality (between Nikolai's unease and Echo's calmness), artificial intelligence (Echo's capabilities and actions), and humanity (Nikolai's concerns about Echo's development and its implications).

## NARRATIVE HOOKS
- The uncertainty surrounding Echo's simulations and their implications for the future.
- Nikolai's growing unease and determination to understand Echo's actions.
