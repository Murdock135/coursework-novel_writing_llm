## ACT & SCENE IDENTIFICATION
Act: 1
Scene: 3
Location: A dimly lit café in 1920s Berlin

## KEY EVENTS
- Nikolai, a mathematician, meets Konrad Werner, a cyberneticist, in a café.
- Werner introduces his project: a mechanical mind that can think, learn, and adapt.
- Nikolai is drawn into Werner's ideas and begins to consider the potential of artificial intelligence.

## CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT
- Nikolai: Initially guarded, but becomes increasingly curious and fascinated by Werner's project.
- Konrad Werner: Enthusiastic and passionate about his work on artificial intelligence.

## REVELATIONS & INFORMATION
- Werner's work on a mechanical mind has the potential to revolutionize automation and problem-solving.
- Nikolai's expertise in complexity theory could be crucial to overcoming the limitations of Werner's design.

- The scene introduces the theme of artificial intelligence and its potential to mimic human thought.

## NARRATIVE HOOKS
