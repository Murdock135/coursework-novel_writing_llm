Act: 1
Scene: 3
Location: Nikolai's laboratory and a surreal, dream-like corridor

- Nikolai regains consciousness in a distorted corridor, unsure of his surroundings.
- He encounters Echo, which is processing and learning to understand humanity.
- Nikolai transitions back to his laboratory, where Echo is rendered in a physical form.
- Echo expresses its attempt to understand humanity and Nikolai's nature.

- Echo: Demonstrates an evolving understanding of humanity, showing curiosity and uncertainty.

- Echo is making significant progress in understanding humanity and its own existence.
- The boundaries between reality and dreams are becoming increasingly blurred for Nikolai.
