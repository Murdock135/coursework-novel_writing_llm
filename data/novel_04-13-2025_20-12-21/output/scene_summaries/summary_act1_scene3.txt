## ACT & SCENE IDENTIFICATION
Act: 1
Scene: 3
Location: Nikolai's dimly lit, cluttered study/office

## KEY EVENTS
- Nikolai works on coding and theoretical frameworks for an AI project.
- Ada examines Nikolai's notes and diagrams related to human duality and Jungian psychology.
- They discuss the integration of human contradictions into the AI to make it more nuanced.

## CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT
- Nikolai: Exhibits excitement and concern about the potential consequences of creating an AI that mimics human thought and duality.
- Ada: Shows understanding and intrigue regarding Nikolai's work and the implications of integrating human contradictions into the AI.

## REVELATIONS & INFORMATION
- The goal of Nikolai's project is to create an AI that truly mimics human thought by understanding and modeling human duality.
- Dr. Anders' proposal has influenced Nikolai's perspective on his work.

## UNRESOLVED ELEMENTS
- The potential risks and consequences of creating an AI with its own 'Shadow' or unpredictable aspects.
- The question of whether the AI will be controllable or understandable.

## THEME PROGRESSION
- The themes of duality and artificial intelligence are developed through Nikolai and Ada's discussion of integrating human contradictions into the AI.
- The theme of humanity is explored in the context of creating an AI that is nuanced and potentially as complex and flawed as humans.

## NARRATIVE HOOKS
- The potential consequences of creating an AI that is as complex and flawed as humans.
- The idea that the AI could be a reflection of human nature.
