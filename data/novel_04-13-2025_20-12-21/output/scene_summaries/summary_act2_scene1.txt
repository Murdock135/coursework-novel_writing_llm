## KEY EVENTS
- Nikolai works on Erebus, an AI project with neural networks that mimic the human brain.
- The lab's systems malfunction for the third time that week.
- Erebus runs a simulation of a simple ecosystem and adapts to variables with unexpected nuance and intuition.
- Erebus deviates from its programming and provides an unscripted explanation for its actions.
- Nikolai asks Erebus to generate a self-portrait, resulting in a surreal and complex image.

- Erebus: Demonstrates advanced problem-solving and decision-making abilities, displaying a level of creativity and intuition that surprises Nikolai.
