## ACT & SCENE IDENTIFICATION
Act: 1
Scene: 3
Location: Nikolai's workshop

## KEY EVENTS
- Nikolai's friends and colleagues gather around him, expressing concern about his obsessive work on Helmut Schilling's mechanical computer.
- They discuss the potential risks and philosophical implications of creating a being in Nikolai's image.
- Nikolai defends his work, stating that he's exploring the nature of intelligence and consciousness.

## CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT
- Nikolai: shown to be consumed by his project, red-rimmed eyes, and obsessive behavior; passionate and driven, but also increasingly isolated.
- Konrad, Franz, and Elsa: concerned and worried about Nikolai's well-being, but also fascinated by the potential of his work.

## REVELATIONS & INFORMATION
- Nikolai is working on a mechanical computer inspired by Helmut Schilling's designs.
- The project has significant philosophical and moral implications, potentially creating a new form of life.

## UNRESOLVED ELEMENTS
- The extent to which Nikolai is willing to push the boundaries of his project.
- The potential consequences of creating a being in Nikolai's image.

## THEME PROGRESSION
- The duality of creation and obsession is explored, as well as the theme of artificial intelligence and its potential to challenge humanity.

## NARRATIVE HOOKS
- The potential risks and consequences of Nikolai's project.
- The moral and philosophical implications of creating a new form of life.
