Act: 1
Scene: 1
Location: Nikolai's workshop

## KEY EVENTS
- Nikolai works on his mechanical computer, driven by a fervent passion to understand human consciousness.
- He recalls warnings from friends and colleagues, including Konrad, who expressed concern about the project's implications.
- Nikolai continues to work, undeterred, as he crafts a machine that could potentially reveal the secrets of human thought.

## CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT
- Nikolai: Exhibits an unrelenting fervor and obsession with his project, driven by a sense of vocation and a desire to transcend human boundaries.
- Konrad and other colleagues: Show concern and trepidation about Nikolai's project, fearing it may lead to unforeseen negative consequences.

## REVELATIONS & INFORMATION
- Nikolai is creating a mechanical computer inspired by Helmut Schilling's designs, with the goal of understanding human consciousness.
- The project has the potential to unveil secrets about the nature of consciousness and blur the distinction between human and machine.

## UNRESOLVED ELEMENTS
- The implications and potential consequences of Nikolai's creation.
- The validity of Konrad's concerns about the project.

## THEME PROGRESSION
- The themes of duality (creator vs. creation, human vs. machine) and artificial intelligence are introduced, with Nikolai's work embodying the tension between these dualities.
- The theme of humanity is explored through Nikolai's obsession and the potential consequences of his creation.

## NARRATIVE HOOKS
- The potential consequences of Nikolai's creation, including the possibility of it unveiling secrets about human consciousness.
- The concerns and warnings from Nikolai's friends and colleagues, which may lead to future conflicts or revelations.
