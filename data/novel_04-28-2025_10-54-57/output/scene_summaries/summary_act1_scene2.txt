## ACT & SCENE IDENTIFICATION
Act: 1
Scene: 3
Location: Nikolai's workshop

## KEY EVENTS
- Nikolai intensely studies the schematics of Helmut Schilling's mechanical computer.
- He contemplates the potential duality of artificial intelligence, merging precision and unpredictability.
- Nikolai becomes convinced that the answer lies in the interplay between code and circuitry.
- He envisions creating a being that combines machine accuracy with human emotion.
- Nikolai draws parallels between his work and the myth of the Golem, questioning whether he's playing God.

## CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT
- Nikolai: consumed by his work, intensely passionate, and driven by a sense of revolutionary discovery.

## REVELATIONS & INFORMATION
- The mechanical computer holds the key to creating a machine that can think.
- The potential for artificial intelligence to embody duality (precision and unpredictability).

## UNRESOLVED ELEMENTS
- The risks and rewards associated with creating a being in one's own image.
- The implications of blurring the distinctions between human and machine.

## THEME PROGRESSION
- The themes of duality and artificial intelligence are advanced through Nikolai's contemplation of creating a machine that embodies both precision and unpredictability.
- The theme of humanity is explored through Nikolai's comparison of his work to the myth of the Golem.

## NARRATIVE HOOKS

- The ethical implications of crafting a being in his own image.
